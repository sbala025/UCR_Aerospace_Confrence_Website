About Us

We aim to inspire our community to innovate new aerospace technologies for the betterment of the world. There are many technologies being implemented into other industries, such as search- and-rescue; data communications; surveying; cinematography; research; and more. With so many advancements, we want our community to be able to leverage them into their own passion projects.

There are already UCR students hard at work in developing and advancing technologies, from building autonomous aircraft to developing liquid-fueled rockets. We can help encourage more of these ground-breaking projects by gathering support and providing an opportunity to learn more about aerospace.
